 BIO FAMILY TWO POINT PROGRAMME
Chandani Padmali Withana a past pupil of Visakha Vidyalaya, Colombo Married to PDG Lion Paddy Withana Mother of two daughters and grandmother to one grand daughter A member of the Lions Club of Aluthgama Bentota for fifteen years Served the Club as President, Secretary, Treasurer, Leo Advisor Served the District as Zone Chairperson, District Chairperson Lions Quest

A Melvin Jones Fellow 2015 Mahendra Amarasuriya Fellow 2018 2nd Vice District Governor 2017 1st Vice District Governor February 2018 District Governor 2018
Chandani Padmali Withana only child of Chandra & Nandani de Silva of Colombo, was educated at Visakha Vidyalaya Colombo. She represented her school at Hockey and was awarded school colours.

Married to Paddy Withana they are the parents of two daughters. Nadeeka a past pupil of Visakha Vidyalaya is a Lawyer by Profession. She is a Bachelor of Arts from the National University of Singapore, Master of Science in Strategic Studies from the Nanyang University of Singapore, LLB from the University of London and an Attorney at Law.

Younger daughter Vindya a past pupil of Visakha Vidyalaya, Bachelor of Science in Business & Information Technology. Married to Nuwan Bandara, a Senior Software Engineer at Depesco USA. They are parents of one daughter Evni Akithya

Both daughters are former Leos the Leo Club of Aluthgama Bentota and Nadeeka is a Member of the Lions Club of Aluthgama Bentota

Chandani, a Lion Lady since 1986 became the Lion Lady of the Year in 1999 / 2000, and a Lion of Aluthgama Bentota in the year 2003. She has held many offices in the Club:-

Secretary 2005 / 2006

President 2006 / 2007, 2007 / 2008

Treasurer 2015 / 2016, 2016 / 2017, 2017 / 2018

Zone Chairperson 2008 / 2009, 2014 / 2015

District Chairperson Lions Quest 2012 /2013

Awarded the Best Zone Chairperson 2014 /2015

Melvin Jones Fellow 2015

Mahendra Amarasuriya Fellow 2018

Her experience in Lionism and its activities was as the spouse of Past District Governor Lion Paddy Withana, through his many functions in the Lions District before becoming a District Governor.

Lion Chandani was elected as the Second Vice District Governor in May 2017, First Vice District Governor in February 2018 and the District Governor in May 2018.
